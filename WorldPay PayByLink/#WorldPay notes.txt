A simple shortcode that creates a payment link within your content. 
The user is directed to a product summary page and then directed to WolrdPay to complete the transaction.



*Upload your logo.png and payment page files under;
WorldPay > Setup > Edit Payment Pages > 'your installation id' > File Management
